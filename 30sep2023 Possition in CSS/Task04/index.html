Principal's Message:

It is a matter of great privilege that I have got the opportunity to perform my duties, as Principal of the prestigious 131-year-old, Government Holkar (Model Autonomous) Science College. The institution was established on 10th June 1891, by His Highness Maharaja Shri Shivajirao Holkar, the erstwhile ruler of Indore. The college has been accredited with ‘A’ grade by NAAC on 19th January 2016, with CGPA 3.23. It is one of the largest Government Science Colleges in the state. The college works under the auspices of the Department of Higher Education, Government of MP and is affiliated to Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya, Indore. DAVV Indore :

The college is committed to fulfil its vision of providing holistic education to the students, where they can grow into sensible, mature and responsible citizens of the country. The faculty members and students strive to give their best in academics, cultural and sports activities. As Principal, I intend to take forward the legacy of my seniors and make this institution reach its zenith. In its quest for excellence, the college focuses on the curriculum by constantly enriching the programmes, by incorporating new and relevant courses, certificate courses that focus on innovative techniques, employability, entrepreneurship development and skill development. The inclusion of Choice-Based Credit System and Elective Paper System at PG and UG level, respectively provide curricular flexibility, thereby fulfilling the local needs in tune with the emerging national and global trends.

Contributes:

The college contributes to sensitise the students, regarding issues such as gender, environmental-sustainability, human-values and professional ethics. We wish to inculcate these values to produce righteous and truthful individuals, who can think logically and critically, with a scientific bent of mind. We want our students to be successful in different fields and contribute in nation-building. The college is aware of its responsibility towards environment conservation. Under the Green Policy, a Solid Waste Management System has been installed to keep the environment clean. The college regularly conducts green and energy audits. The Butterfly Park adds uniqueness to the lush green gardens of the college. The institution has an excellent infrastructure, to meet the escalating strength of the students. There are two Academic Blocks and other buildings with spacious, well-furnished classrooms. Smart-Boards have been installed in all the classrooms. The faculty members take up interactive, instructional techniques and provide quality learning through the use of ICT resources, Learning Management System (LMS) and other e-resources. They take initiative to keep abreast with the latest technological and academic innovations, which further add to institutional excellence. The College encourages and promotes research projects useful to the humanity and to fulfil this it has a Research Wing, two independent new blocks with well-equipped and sophisticated laboratories. The faculty members provide consultancy to external agencies with expertise on specific knowledge. The college has signed MoUs and has collaborations with national and international institutions.
